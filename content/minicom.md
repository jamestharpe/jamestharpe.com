## Minicom

[**Minicom**](https://salsa.debian.org/minicom-team/minicom) is a text-based modem control program for [Linux](linux.md) and other unix-like [operating systems](operating-systems.md).

### How to Exit Minicom

`Ctrl+A`, `x`.
